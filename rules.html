How to play

  1. This is a one player tetris game

  2. The control keys are;
    • A : Move Left
    • D : Move Right
    • S : Move Down
    • E : Rotate

  3. You can only move your pieces in specific ways

  4. Each row occupies 12 tetrimino

  5. You can only remove pieces from the screen by filling all the blank space on the row

  6. With completion of a row you score 10

  7. Your game is over if your tetrimino piece reach the top of the screen
